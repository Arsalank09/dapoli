01 · About Dapoli

A coastal hill station
steeped in history

  • A town in Ratnagiri district, Maharashtra — and a celebrated coastal hill station.
  • Once “Camp Dapoli,” where the British set their cantonment camps.
  • Separated from the Sahyadri range — bordered by Khed to the east, Mandangad to the north, Guhaghar & Chiplun to the south, and the Arabian Sea to the west.
  • Blessed with a sweeping 50 km seaboard.
